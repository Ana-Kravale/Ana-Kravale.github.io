Minecraft has too many mobs to count.

Passive Mobs, will never attack you, like cats, cows, sheep, and couple of more.
Neutral Mobs, won't harm you as long as you don't bother them too, like bees, wolfs, enderman, panda, etc.
Hostile Mobs & Bosses, despise you and want you dead. Examples are...zombies, skeletons, creepers, dragon..the list goes on and on.

This page includes some of most favourable, and most hated mobs of minecraft.

LIKED BY MANY:

Enderman. This tall shy mob is adored by many. It is shy to the point where it will try to kill you if you look it straight in the eyes. Although endermen like to randomly steal your blocks you used to build your beautiful house, it is very cute and likes to talk to you. Listen carefully to what it got to say
Husk. A special zombie found in the desert. It is slow and takes a while to catch up with the player. The awful noise it makes while gasping for air sends shivers down the spine.
Wolf. We all love dogs, and when we cannot get one irl, why not tame one in minecraft. A wolf becomes a dog in this game once you tame it with enough bones, which ou can get by killing skeletons. The fun part is that you can have as many dogs as youd like in minecraft.. you just got to find them... and fight a lot of skeletons. Along with being adorable, this mob will not hesitate to protect you from any mob thats attacking you. Of course it can't win against them all, so you might want to protect it by making it sit the fight out, while you deal with the intruder.
Axolotl. Recently added to the game. It can be seen in different colours, pink, brown, yellow, while, and in the rarest colour..blue. You can catch one with a bucket of water. Everyone can agree that these little guys were a good update to the game.
Fox. Especially the baby fox. This cute creature is hard to see upclose, it runs away as soon as it senses your presense. The rarest fox is the white fox, which could be found in the snowy biomes or mountains.

HATED BY ALL:

Baby Zombie. It's small and fast. Giving everyone a jumpscare when it pops up out of nowhere or around the corner. Because of its small size, its almost impossible to kill it first hit. Although it's a baby, the damage is done almost twice worse than a regular zombie. Unlike other mobs, you cannot run away from this little guy, so your only options are to face it, or hide in a house until it turns daytime and the mob burns to crisp.
Skeleton. Although just bones, and no eyes, this mob can shoot arrows pretty well, it almost never misses the player. You are lucky if you managed to craft yourself a shield before encountering one of these.
Creeper. Don't be fooled by its innocent look. This guy does not care about the flowers on your front yard, or your beautiful mansion and your pets, it will follow you quietly until you are close enough to it and then...BOOM! You almost never have the chance to survive, the shield might protect you, if you are fast enough. It's even worse when you are exploring underground and mining until you suddenly hear the hissing right in your ear.
Zombie Pigman. This is not the worst mob to encounter, but certainly one of the worst mobs to annoy. Hitting it on purpose or by accident, will not stop it and ALL the zombie pigmen around it from run straight at you. Forgiveness is out the question.
